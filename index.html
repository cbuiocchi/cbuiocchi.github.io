Deckborn Prototype.exe

Deckborn is an RPG with deckbuilding combat — a bigger-scope take on the genre inspired by classic JRPGs like Final Fantasy 7. It's a solo project built in Godot, with a basic turn-based card combat system and a small overworld to explore. Currently in production, targeting a polished vertical slice by Fall 2026.

Wrong Bird Training Loop.exe

“Penguins can't fly” — some idiot. Wrong Bird is a pet raising simulator with roguelite mechanics in which you train penguins to fly. Built to learn UI/UX and RNG-driven systems; the training-loop prototype was completed in May 2026, with a distance-launching loop and full vertical slice targeted for Fall 2026.

About Me.txt - Notepad

Hey, I'm Clay (they/them). I'm a game designer working on games I've always wanted to play but nobody's made yet.

Right now I'm wrapping up Circuit Stream's Game Design Bootcamp. I am currently working on two major projects: Deckborn, a 3D deckbuilding JRPG built in Unreal Engine 5, and Wrong Bird, a penguin training-and-launching sim.

What I care about most is game design: systems, UI/UX, and level design, and making sure all of it actually feels good to play.

Before games, I studied music and psychology. I also work as an early education teacher these days, and I live near Boston.
